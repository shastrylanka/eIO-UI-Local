US Trade Sanctions & Embargoes

Products and services that, directly or indirectly, originate from, are provided to or within, or facilitate business to or with a country subject to U.S. embargo or trade sanctions, unless authorized by US law, are prohibited. These countries currently include: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Sudan and Syria. This list may change due to updates to the export laws. Compliance with the most current list is required. For additional information, consult the US Commerce and Treasury Department websites at: http://www.bis.doc.gov and http://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/sanctions/Programs/Pages/Programs.aspx. Please note that this would prohibit clients (including airlines) whose campaigns feature Cuba as a destination from advertising with us.

 

Social Media Landing Pages

Ads may link to a "public" social networking account page (e.g. Facebook, MySpace, and Twitter) that is controlled by the advertiser, provided the ad is otherwise compliant with our policies. Any Ads that contain Facebook platform functionality (e.g. Like Button, Share Button etc) will be prohibited. Ads will not be prohibited if they do not contain platform functionality but link directly to the advertisers' controlled page (e.g. fan page) without any sign-in or registration requirement.
